FAQ

  • Is IC Markets regulated?
    IC Markets operates under different entities. Verify the exact entity and license for your country on the regulator’s register.
  • What are the main IC Markets fees?
    Main costs are spreads and, on raw accounts, commissions. If you hold trades overnight, include swaps/financing.
  • How can I test withdrawals safely?
    Complete KYC first, deposit a small amount, then withdraw back to the same method and record processing time before scaling.
