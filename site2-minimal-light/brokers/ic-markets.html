IC Markets Review

Often chosen by active traders for competitive pricing and multiple platform options. Confirm entity and fees for your region.

Quick verdict

Best for active traders who care about spreads/commissions and want MT4/MT5/cTrader options (where available).

Regulation (example)

Regulation can vary by entity. Always verify the company and license for your account jurisdiction.

Platforms

MT4, MT5, cTrader (availability varies by region/entity).

Pros

  • Competitive pricing on major pairs (account dependent)
  • Good platform selection for active traders
  • Suitable for higher-frequency trading styles

Cons

  • Conditions vary by entity and region
  • Non-trading fees can apply depending on method
  • Always confirm instruments and account requirements

Who it’s best for

Intermediate/advanced traders who focus on costs, execution, and platform flexibility.

What to check before signing up

  • Your exact regulated entity and protections
  • Spreads/commissions and any volume-based discounts
  • Deposit/withdrawal methods, fees, and processing time
