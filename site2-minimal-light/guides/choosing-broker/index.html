How to Choose a Forex Broker

A simple checklist to avoid bad brokers and focus on what actually matters: safety, costs, and reliability.

1) Regulation & entity

Confirm the legal entity you’ll open an account with and verify its license on the regulator’s website.

2) Total costs

Look at spreads + commissions, plus swaps/financing if you hold trades overnight.

3) Withdrawals & support

Test a small deposit/withdrawal on a demo or small live account. Fast support matters when something breaks.

Red flags

  • Unclear entity or missing license details
  • Withdrawal complaints with no clear resolution
  • Aggressive bonus marketing or pressure to deposit
