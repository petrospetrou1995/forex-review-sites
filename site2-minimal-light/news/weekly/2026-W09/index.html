Weekly market brief (LATAM) — Week 9 (2026)

A practical weekly plan for LATAM traders: broker checks, forex catalysts, and risk rules.

2026-W09

Broker checklist

  • Verify the exact regulated entity for your LATAM country.
  • Compare all-in costs (spreads + commission + swaps).
  • Test withdrawals early; track fees and settlement time.

Forex catalysts

  • Inflation and rate expectations (local + US) drive FX volatility.
  • Watch USD/MXN and USD/BRL liquidity windows for spread changes.

Risk rules

  • Set a daily loss cap and stop when hit.
  • Reduce size first when volatility spikes.
