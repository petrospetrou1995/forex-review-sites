Quick answer

Pick a broker that is regulated for your specific entity, offers transparent all-in costs, and supports reliable withdrawals for your country. Then do a small withdrawal test before scaling up.

  • Verify the exact legal entity for your country (it affects protections and dispute paths).
  • Compare total trading costs: spread + commission + swaps (if you hold overnight).
  • Check deposit/withdraw rails, processing times, and currency conversion fees.

  • Do brokers offer the same regulation in every LATAM country?
